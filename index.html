Handcrafted ceramic objects,
inspired by nature

In our work, we explore the spontaneous effects of fire and natural materials, creating warm, timeless designs for interiors and everyday objects.

We take a hands-on approach, favoring raku and wood-fired saggar techniques, embracing ancient methods to make each piece unique, with its own story.

Inspired by nature, we aim to reconnect people with the natural world through carefully handcrafted pieces—from design and shaping to firing and finishing.

ABOUT US

I work on the potter’s wheel, constantly experimenting with shapes, special firings like raku and saggar, and surface textures, seeking unique, elegant, timeless, and authentic forms."

Silvia Farina

"I am a sculptor and visual artist. In my work, I explore forms of forest and other animal life, as well as trees and creatures with which I feel a deep connection. For Saba, I work as a designer and decorator, also handling firings and marketing."
